#  WealthWise

**WealthWise** is a personal finance management application designed to help individuals take control of their money with clarity and confidence. Whether you're tracking daily expenses or planning your monthly budget, WealthWise provides a simple and structured way to understand your financial habits.

---

##  Overview

Managing money can feel like trying to hold water in your hands — it slips through unnoticed. WealthWise solves this by giving users a centralized platform to:

* Track daily and monthly expenses
* Monitor income streams
* Categorize spending habits
* Gain insights into financial behavior

The goal is to transform scattered transactions into meaningful financial awareness.

---

##  Objectives

### Main Objective

To develop an intuitive and efficient system that enables users to manage and monitor their personal finances effectively.

### Specific Objectives

* To allow users to record income and expenses
* To categorize transactions for better tracking
* To provide a clear overview of financial activity
* To support better budgeting decisions

---

##  Features

*  Expense and income tracking
*  Categorization of transactions
*  Daily and monthly financial monitoring
*  User authentication system
*  Data storage using MongoDB

---

##  Chatbot Integration (Current Status)

The system design included an AI-powered chatbot intended to assist users with financial queries and guidance. However, due to technical limitations during development, the chatbot functionality was not successfully implemented at this stage.

Future improvements will focus on refining and integrating this feature to enhance user interaction and provide intelligent financial assistance.

---

##  Limitations

* Chatbot feature not fully functional
* Limited advanced analytics and reporting
* Basic UI implementation (subject to improvement)

---

##  Future Enhancements

* Fully functional AI chatbot assistant
* Advanced financial insights and analytics
* Mobile-friendly interface
* Notifications and reminders for budgeting

---

##  Conclusion

WealthWise is a step toward smarter financial living. While still evolving, it lays a strong foundation for helping users build better money habits and make informed financial decisions.
